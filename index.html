BOOKING PROCESS

1

Fill out the form we made for you to kickstart the process for your next tattoo. Each request is reviewed personally to make sure we fully understand your vision.

2

You'll receive a follow-up message to clarify your concept, confirm the style, and provide a personalized quote based on the design's complexity and estimated time.

3

Once you're ready to move forward, a non-refundable deposit is required to lock in your appointment. This goes toward your final cost and secures your design time.

4

A custom design will be prepared based on the details you provide. Minor adjustments can be made before or on the day of your session to make sure everything feels just right.

5

Arrive on time, well-rested, and hydrated. You'll walk in with an idea — and walk out with permanent art made just for you.
